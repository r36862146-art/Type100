# Type100 Vision

## Mission

Build the fastest, cleanest, and most enjoyable typing practice platform.

## Vision

Type100 helps users improve typing speed and accuracy through a beautiful,
minimal, distraction-free experience.

## Target Audience

- Students
- Government exam aspirants
- Professionals
- Casual learners

## Initial Languages

- English
- Hindi

## Core Principles

- Fast
- Simple
- Premium
- Accessible
- Mobile First
- Responsive
- Performance Focused

## What Type100 is NOT

- Not a social network
- Not an LMS
- Not a gaming platform
- Not an AI tutor
- Not a certificate platform

Every feature should improve typing practice.
